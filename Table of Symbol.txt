$\mathcal(W)$ & Grid World\\
$h$ & Height of the Grid World\\
$l$ & Length of the Grid World\\
$c$ & Position of a cell in the form (x, y)\\
$E_c$ & Energy Cell in position c\\
$E_{c_t}$ & Energy Stored Energy Cell at time t\\
$E_{c_{max}}$ & Maximum Energy Level of the Energy Cell\\
$E_{c_{reg}}$ & Regenaration Rate of the Energy Cell\\
$E_{c_{max}}$ & Minimum Critical Energy Level of the Energy Cell\\
$I$ & Individual\\
$\mathcal(A)$ & Actions Set\\
$\mathcal(B)$ & Behaviors Set\\
$I_a$ & Age of an Individual\\
$I_{a_{max}}$ & Maximum Age of an Individual\\
$I_{a_{mat}}$ & Maturity Age of an Individual\\
$I_p$ & Position of an Individual on the Grid World\\
$I_e$ & Energy Level of an Individual\\
$I_{e_{max}}$ & Maximum Energy Level of an Individual\\
$I_{e_n}$ & Energy Needed of an Individual\\
$I_{e_e}$ & Extra Energy of an Individual\\
$I_{e_r}$ & Energy Requested of an Individual\\
$I_{S_p}$ & Social Parameters of an Individual\\
$I_r$ & Radius of an Individual\\
$I_{M_r}$ & Mutation Rate of an Individual\\
$I_{idx}$ & Index of an Individual\\
$I_{L}$ & Last Action Taken of an Individual\\
$I_{B}$ & Behavior of an Individual\\
$C$ & Cost\\
$S_s$ & Selfishness Parameter\\
$S_a$ & Altruism Parameter\\
$S_n$ & Normality Parameter\\
